OUR STORY

Chaukiss LLC is a family-owned European subsidiary out of a small town in Czech Republic. Founded in 2017 by Filip, Chaukiss is the new modern designer and manufacturer of greeting cards and stationery items made from sustainable materials.

In 2022, we are expanding our distribution to America. Get in touch to learn more.
